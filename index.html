Blackjack Game

Rules

1. The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

2. Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand.

3. Each player starts with two cards, and one of the dealer's cards is hidden.

4. Click 'Hit' to get another card and 'Stand' to stop.

5. The dealer will reveal their hidden card and must hit until the value reaches 17 or higher.

6. If you go over 21, you bust, and the dealer wins.

7. If the dealer busts, and you don't, you win.

8. If you and the dealer both don't bust, whoever is closer to 21 wins.
